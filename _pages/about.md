<i class="fas fa-quote-left fa-2x fa-pull-left fa-border" aria-hidden="true"></i>
Linshuai Zhang is an Associate Professor and Master's Supervisor at Chengdu University of Traditional Chinese Medicine. He received his Ph.D. in Intelligent Mechanical Systems Engineering from Kagawa University, Japan, and completed postdoctoral research at Kagawa University. His research focuses on vascular interventional surgical robots, acupuncture robots, haptic feedback, intelligent sensing, and interactive control for medical robotic systems.
